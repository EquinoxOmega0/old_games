the keyeditor



Everyone imagines something else for the optimal keyboard configuration. For change the keys you must click on "edit keys" in the main menu. After this you have to click on "reorganize keys". The name of the order to edit appears. If you want to change it, you'll have to press the new key on the keeyboard else click on "OK". Don't forget: after pressing the new key you must click on "OK" too. After editing all keys the text "ready to save" appears and then you must click on "save changes" to save. To get the original configuration back, you must first click on "default settings" and then on "save changes". To leave the keyeditor you must click on "close".
