Starfight is a 2D Spacebattelgame. You can either play against an other human player or against the computer. There are 12 different ships with a wide varity of equipement. Futhermore you have a mapeditor to create your own playing worlds.


system requirements:


3,5 MB harddisc
4 MB Ram
500 MHz Pentium II or higher
screen resolution of min. 800x600
soundcard+boxes
Windows 95 or higher
