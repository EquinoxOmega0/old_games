Manuel




After the start of the program you get into the main menu. Your are only able to start a game after you've chosen a ship. Player 1 uses the left mousebuttom to click at the image of the ship. After you've done this, it will get a yellow frame. Player 2 does the same with the right mousebuttom and his ship will get a turkis frame. Of course both can chose the same ship. If you've engaged a Computerplayer with the "Computerplayer off/Computerplayer on"-buttom, you would have been Player 1 and the computer choses his ship by hisself. If this buttom shows "Computerplayer on" you are playing against the computer else not. For changing its state, you've to click it. After all players have chosen their ships you are able to start the game by clicking "start game". To quit the play you've to press the Esc-key and to break it the Break-key. With "reset score" you change the score of the player to zero. If you want to change the keys, you will have to click on "edit keys" and to get help you must simply click on "HELP". The buttom "quit game" was created to shut down the game. On the right hand-side of the main menu there are some buttoms to edit the game. With the mapeditor you can create your own maps for game. The other buttoms control some mapobjects and gamefeatures: "wormhole on/off", "events on/off" (supernova, spezial computerenemy and astroidshower), "sun on/off", "nebula on/off", "astroids on/off", "black hole on/off", "anomalie on/off" and "spezials on/off".
